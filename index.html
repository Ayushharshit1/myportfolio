01 — About

Engineer by discipline,
defender by curiosity.

I'm Ayush Harshit, a Computer Science Engineering student building at the intersection of full-stack development and cybersecurity. I like software that does two things well at once: work reliably for the people using it, and hold up under the people trying to break it.

Most of my recent time has gone into ThreatLens, an enterprise threat-intelligence platform I helped architect end-to-end — from API integrations and container orchestration to the interfaces analysts actually use. Alongside that, I'm teaching myself machine learning to understand where automated defense is headed next, and contributing to open source along the way.

I enjoy the unglamorous parts of engineering as much as the exciting ones: clear documentation, sane architecture, and code reviews that leave a codebase better than they found it.
